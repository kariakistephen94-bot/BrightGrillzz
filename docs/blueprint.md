# **App Name**: BrightGrillzz Elite

## Core Features:

- Luxury AI Concierge: An AI-powered tool that acts as a personal dining assistant, suggesting perfect protein and drink pairings from the menu based on the user's taste preferences and dietary needs.
- Interactive Digital Sommelier: A visually-stunning digital menu specifically designed for mobile devices featuring high-resolution cinematography of the signature grills and seafood platters.
- Seamless Table Booking: A custom reservation engine that stores customer data and booking preferences, integrating directly with Google Calendar.
- Priority VIP Hub: A member-exclusive area to capture lead generation, allowing customers to sign up for anniversary offers and early access to new menu launches.
- High-Conversion Checkout: An optimized mobile ordering system for pickup and delivery, ensuring users can place high-value orders in under three clicks.
- Real-time WhatsApp Connect: Floating integration that connects premium clients directly to the restaurant manager for bespoke requests or corporate booking inquiries.
- Customer Data Ledger: Secure storage of customer profiles, booking history, and contact details to facilitate personalized marketing via Firestore.

## Style Guidelines:

- The palette is anchored in Navy Blue (#001F3F) to convey trust, power, and masculine elegance, contrasted against pure White (#FFFFFF) for a clean, editorial aesthetic.
- Deep Red (#C1121F) is used sparingly as an accent for call-to-action buttons to stimulate appetite and create a high-contrast sense of luxury.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern high-contrast serif that feels expensive and high-end. Body font: 'PT Sans' for clarity and warmth on mobile devices.
- A cinematic, immersive layout featuring a vertical scrolling flow that prioritizes large-scale food photography over dense text blocks.
- Thin-line, minimalist icons with a subtle gold gradient to accentuate the premium 'Elite' positioning of the brand.
- Refined, parallax scroll effects on food imagery and smooth, decelerated fade-ins for text sections to evoke a sense of quiet luxury.
